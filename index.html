Baking and Development

I started to bake in Nov 2015. I love this craft and it inspires me a lot in my daily work:

  • you feed people you love;
  • each time, you ship on day 1;
  • you improve your techniques by doing, again and again;
  • your practice pushes you to learn theory;
  • you aim at perfection by doing it wrong.
